Pull Up and Pull Down Circuits

Learn More:
  • Pull-up/pull-down resistors are used in door switches, brake pedal switches, and sensor circuits.
  • They prevent ECU inputs from floating when switches are open.
  • ECUs can reliably detect ON/OFF status thanks to these resistors.
Key Takeaways:
  • A pull-up resistor makes an open switch read HIGH.
  • A pull-down resistor makes an open switch read LOW.
  • These circuits are everywhere in automotive electronics!
